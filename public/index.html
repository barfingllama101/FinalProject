Cowabunga, Baby

It's feeding time.

Feed a hungry cow. Listen to music. What's better than that? An unhungry cow. Play this game designed by your Spotify songs and collect food before the song ends.

Collect Food

In this running simulator, the cow will catch as many treats as possible while avoiding obstacles before the song ends.

Avoid the haybales

Who would want hay when you can get TREATS?!

Listen to your favorite tracks

Cowabunga Baby uses Spotify API to generate the levels. Sign in to your Spotify account when prompted, play a song, and play the game!
