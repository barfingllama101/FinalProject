Proposal

What's in the game?
Story
It's Feeding Time
On your way to give the cow some treats, you dropped the entire BASKET of treats throughout the farm! This cow needs to collect all of the treats before the farmer catches him!
Mechanics

Moving Cow


The player will play as the cow jumping over obstacles and jumping to obtain treats.

Collect Food


The more food the cow collects, the faster he will be.

Obstacles


The obstacles will be placed randomly when the beat gets loudest.

Level Generation


The level will generate based on the Spotify song the player is listening to or by selecting a random song on Spotify.

Starting Off


Players will start at the corner of the screen with navigation instructions. They will click the spacebar to begin.
Controls
SpaceBar to jump, right arrow to move
Aesthetics
2d pixel game much like Stardew Valley
Platform
A web based runner game
